Hello everyone!

My name is Maria J, and I’m an ESL tutor originally from New York, USA.

I received my Bachelor’s Degree in 1986 in Biological Sciences from Fordham University and later that year relocated to live in London UK.

I settled in the UK, although I spent 9 months living, working and travelling in Australia in 1989. In 1990 I obtained a certificate for TEFL from International House in London and then worked teaching English at a language school in Edgware, London for 18 months.

I also taught private students in their homes for 2 years, and later taught UK school children privately for a year.

From 1998 until 2012 I homeschooled my 2 daughters from the ages of 3 and 4 until they both entered university.

In May 2018 I relocated from the UK to live in Las Vegas, Nevada and I started teaching ESL online in August 2018. I relocated again in June 2020 to northern California. Since 2018 I have worked for First Future and Boxfish teaching Chinese children, and for Cambly, Lingoda and Preply teaching adults from numerous countries around the globe. I now teach adults only.

I love meeting so many students from all around the world and having the opportunity to teach from the comfort of my home. To date, I have taught private students from: Austria, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bolivia, Brazil, China, Ecuador, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Israel, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Peru, Russia, Spain, Taiwan, Turkey, and Ukraine. I’m looking forward to meeting more students from some new countries!
