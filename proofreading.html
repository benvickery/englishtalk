PROOFREADING SERVICES

In addition to my ESL services, I am also available for one-off tasks proofreading, creating or editing any written piece you might need assistance with. I have been working as a content editor for Fluentize since June 2019, in addition to regularly assisting my private students with a variety of proofreading and content construction tasks. I am a natural proofreader; I spot errors everywhere and automatically correct them in my head. I have done so since adolescence having been raised by an English teacher mother. I have been proofing all manner of texts for friends and family on a casual basis for years.

I am available for any proofreading or content creation task, from anything such as a short business email up to a written essay or report up to a maximum of approximately 10,000 words (this can be negotiated). My minimum charge is $35 for anything up to 500 words. Any length above this count will be charged at a rate of $50 per hour worked. The number of hours I will require to complete the proofreading will depend on the level of English of the writer, along with degree of difficulty in determining meaning and context, frequency of errors and other deciding factors. I can provide an approximate total cost after reviewing your document prior to commencement of work. Feel free to contact me with your proofreading or content creation request.
